Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13 July 2026

JKEE Automation is an internal automation system used by JKEE Enterprise to manage TikTok Shop order-related notifications.

We may process limited order and customer service information from TikTok Shop, including order ID, package ID, shipment status, conversation ID, and related order notification details.

This information is used only for the following purposes:

  1. To identify when an order has been shipped.
  2. To send order and delivery-related messages to customers.
  3. To provide customers with access to their TikTok Shop logistics or tracking card.
  4. To prevent duplicate shipment notifications.
  5. To troubleshoot automation errors.

We do not sell customer information. We do not use customer information for unrelated marketing outside the TikTok Shop order process.

Data may be processed through TikTok Shop Open API and internal automation tools such as n8n. Access is limited to authorized team members only.
